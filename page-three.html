Destinations

Dubai's destinations are very captivating and attract our attention which shows how amazing they are and how much of a hold it has on the people.

Destinations you need to visit in Dubai

Burj Khalifa

The Burj khalifa is notorious around the world and is probably if not the most famous landmark in Dubai. To it's clean structure and impressive height it surely does stand as a symbolic beacon for the middle east. The Burj Khalifa is known for being the tallest skyscraper in the world and it has broken many records with it's unique architectural design and the skyskraper stands at staggering 828 metres tall (2,717ft). The skyscraper opened in 2010 and ever since then many tourists have travelled to Dubai to see the building and this has made Dubai the most popular tourist destination.

Dubai Mall

The Dubai shopping mall is one of the biggest shopping malls in the world and this has become a great thing for Dubai because many tourists come to visit and buy clothing from high end stores such as Luis Vuitton and Gucci and many other stores and also eat at traditional restaurants so they can get a feel of the Dubai culture. The tourists will also be able to view the different buildings outside and also the sea that flows through the city wich will make it an unforgettable experience.

Dubai Fountain

The Dubai fountain is a fountain system thats located in the Burj Khalifa lake and it is like no other. From the lights, to the music and the water it is truly a magical spectacle the length of the fountain is around 900ft making it the tallest fountain in the world in length. There are also times when Dubai do a fountain show which is truly a magnificent show if your able to watch it. What makes the Dubai fountain unique and beautiful to see is that the fountain is able to move to different melodies to songs which is truly memorable and this would leave the tourists mesmerised.

Dubai Frame

The Dubai Frame is a monumental building located in Zabeel park. It holds the record for the largest frame building in the world. The height of the building is 150.24 metres and a width of 95.53 metres. This building is among the most impressive buildings ever created because no country has built a frame before and this makes it a great landmark and tourist attraction becuase this will make you want to visit it. The Dubai Frame opened in 2013 and since then many tourists have had the pleasure of visiting it and have also gone inside and I guarantee you'll not want to miss out.
