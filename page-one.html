Activities

A city that's made for people having amazing experiences, Dubai has an incredible line of different activites that would leave tourists in awe and down below you will see the many memorable activities that they offer

Activities to do in Dubai

Jetskiing

Enjoy the ride of a lifetime with our fast paced Jet ski rides, you'll be able to ride around the beautiful Palm Jumeirah and also the Burj Al Arab and take stunning photos in front of them.

Sandboarding

You will be taken by instructors to the Dubai desert where you will have the opportunity to sandboard on the high hills of the desert which will provide a fun experience and there will also be lunch included.

Sky Diving

Experience a breathtaking sky diving experience with highly trained sky diving instructors and also given the opportunity to dive above either the amazing Palm Jumeirah or Dubai desert.

Dune buggying

Be taken to the famous Dubai desert where you will be able to get your hands on a Dune Buggy where you will ride around the desert with friends or family looking at the desert landscape.

Waterpark

Dubai is home to the largest waterpark in the world and also many others, they provide exciting and enjoyable experiences becuase they are packed with many slides and also showcase many marine animals which will provide the best experience.

Aquarium

The Dubai aquarium is housed to thousands of aquatic animals which include over 140 species, The tourists/visitors will be able to come face to face with the animals which will leave them having a long lasting experience that they will never forget.
