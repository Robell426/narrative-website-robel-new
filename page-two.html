Hotels

Dubai is known for having lavish and magnificent hotels and this is what persuades the tourists to come visit Dubai and stay in these luxurious hotels and down below you will see the most popular ones

Hotels you might want to stay at

Burj Al Arab

The Burj Al Arab is the only 7 star hotel in the world and the most iconic hotel in the world and this hotel sure does represent arabian luxury. It's managed and developed by Jumeirah and its known to be the tallest hotel in the world which makes it a notorious one. Many celebrities and Billionaires around the world stay in this hotel and this made it global around the world. It was also built on its own island which made it stand out the most and it also has a helicopter pad on top of the building which makes it rather unique. You will be able to view the different other hotels and you will also be able to view the beautiful Arabian Gulf.

Atlantis, The Palm Hotel

The Atlantis, The Palm is everyones favourite hotel because it's located in front of the beach and when people look out of their windows all thier going to see is an amzing view of the Dubai coastline. The hotel itself is an ocean themed resort that has so many tourists visiting and staying in it every year and one thing to say is that this place will not disapoint you in the slightest. The hotel includes uniquely designed rooms and suites and also views of the beautiful Arabian sea which will have you gazing in awe. If your feeling adventurous also you will be able to take your children to the passageways of 'The Lost Chambers Aquarium' and there are around 65,000 marine animals in the Ambassador Lagoon and this will leave the kids having an amazing experience.

Jumeirah beach Hotel

The Jumeirah beach Hotel has a rather unique architectural design like a wave design but this is quite a clever idea. You will also have the pleasure in viewing the beautiful Arabian Gulf from your room which will make your experience in the hotel quite exciting. There is also a kids club inside where all the children will be able to get involved in activities such as face-painting and mask-making. The adults will also be able to get a spa session in where you will be able to get a massage and also do some aromatherapy which will be beneficial for your psychological wellbeing.

Raffles, The Palm Hotel

Resting on the Dubai shores sits the luxuious resort known as the Raffles, The Palm Hotel. From the lavish rooms to the spacious suites and beautiful villas this will make you feel like your living like the royal family. Having a 24 hour butler service to having expensive Italian Frencesco Molon home furnishing this is next level. There is also different facilities that will be open to guests and these are a fully equiped gym, a spa session, a yoga studio and an indoor swimming pool. This hotel is also located on the edge of the Palm Jumeirah and the hotel is only 5-7 minutes away from the largest waterpark in the world which is 'Aquaventure Waterpark' so families will be able to drive down and enjoy themselves.
