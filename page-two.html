Hotels

Dubai is known for having lavish and magnificent hotels and this is what persuades the tourists to come visit Dubai and stay in these luxurious hotels and down below you will see the most popular ones.

Hotels you might want to stay at

Burj Al Arab

The Burj Al Arab is the only 7 star hotel in the world and the most iconic hotel in the world and this hotel sure does represent arabian luxury. It's managed and developed by Jumeirah and its known to be the tallest hotel in the world which makes it a notorious one. Many celebrities and Billionaires around the world stay in this hotel and this made it global around the world. It was also built on its own island which made it stand out the most and it also has a helicopter pad on top of the building which makes it rather unique. You will be able to view the different other hotels and you will also be able to view the beautiful Arabian Gulf.
